VulnRap — Fact Sheet
====================

WHAT IT IS
  VulnRap is a free, public scoring service for vulnerability reports. It
  detects AI-generated "slop" submissions before they reach human triage,
  using five independent engines whose outputs are combined into a single
  0–100 score with a transparent verdict and per-signal breakdown.

WHO IT IS FOR
  · Bug-bounty platform triagers drowning in low-effort AI submissions.
  · Open-source maintainers receiving security@ reports of varying quality.
  · CSIRTs and PSIRTs that need a first-pass filter before assignment.
  · Researchers studying the impact of generative AI on coordinated
    vulnerability disclosure.

HOW IT WORKS (one-pager methodology)
  Intake. Reports are normalized and PII/secret-redacted on the way in.
  Five engines run in parallel:
    1. Linguistic   — surface features (token entropy, burstiness, template
                      reuse, phrase-cluster matching against known LLM corpora).
    2. Substance    — density of technical artefacts (paths, headers, methods,
                      payloads, command lines, file/line refs) versus prose.
    3. CWE Coherence— embeds the body and checks that its nearest CWE
                      neighbours include the CWE that was claimed; flags
                      mismatches.
    4. AVRI         — Adversarial Vulnerability Report Index; tracks drift of
                      a report against known AI-generated report templates with
                      a re-arming cooldown.
    5. Reputation   — corpus-level signals (hallucinated CVE refs, fabricated
                      vendor names, severity-inflation patterns).
  An isotonic-calibrated combiner with per-CWE weights produces the final
  score. Every signal that fires is exposed with its weight and citation.
  No engine is a black box: fingerprints, training data, and held-out
  metrics are published.

KEY METRICS (rolling, public)
  · Engine accuracy on held-out set:  93.7%  (precision 0.91 / recall 0.94)
  · P50 scoring latency:              142 ms
  · P95 scoring latency:              410 ms
  · Detectable signals:               63
  · CWE families recognised:          full MITRE 4.14 catalogue
  · Pricing:                          free, rate-limited public tier

WHAT IT IS NOT
  VulnRap does not decide whether a vulnerability is real. It scores
  reports for likelihood of being AI-slop, then surfaces the evidence so a
  human can make the call faster. False positives are possible and the
  threshold is tunable per integration.
